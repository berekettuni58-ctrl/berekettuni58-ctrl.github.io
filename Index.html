Artificial Intelligence Types & Functions

Complete Guide to Modern AI Technologies

Reactive Machines

Basic AI
  • Respond to current scenarios only
  • No memory of past experiences
  • Excellent for specific tasks
  • Examples: Chess-playing AI, Spam filters

Limited Memory AI

Intermediate AI
  • Learn from historical data
  • Make informed decisions
  • Used in self-driving cars
  • Examples: Chatbots, Voice assistants

Theory of Mind AI

Advanced AI
  • Understand human emotions
  • Social interaction capabilities
  • Predict behavior and intentions
  • Future technology

Self-Aware AI

Super AI
  • Human-level consciousness
  • Self-awareness and emotions
  • Independent decision making
  • Theoretical concept

Machine Learning

Practical AI
  • Learn from data patterns
  • Improve performance over time
  • Used in recommendations
  • Examples: Netflix, Amazon suggestions

Neural Networks

Deep Learning
  • Mimic human brain structure
  • Image and speech recognition
  • Complex pattern detection
  • Examples: Face recognition, Medical diagnosis
